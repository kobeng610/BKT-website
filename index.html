About BKT

BKT Consultants, Inc. brings over 50 years of combined expertise, offering customized training that aligns with each country's distinct governance landscape. With a strong foundation in leadership, communication, legislative processes, and accountability, BKT also provides specialized workshops on gender-sensitive budgeting, public financial management, and ethics.

What sets BKT apart is its unmatched expertise in governance capacity-building. With years of experience training professionals across various sectors, BKT ensures that its programs are tailored to each country’s unique governance environment, delivering practical, impactful solutions. The firm’s proven success is evident in its longstanding partnerships with governance experts, former parliamentarians, government agencies, and prestigious American universities, all contributing to measurable improvements in governance practices.

As a US-based consultancy, BKT upholds the highest standards of integrity, inclusivity, and innovation, ensuring that each training program enhances legislative and policymaking skills, strengthens transparency and ethical practices, and builds public trust and accountability. Through its commitment to excellence, BKT stands ready to support your strategic development goals with customized training solutions that drive meaningful change.

Our Mission Statement

To empower parliamentarians and government officials with the knowledge, skills, and tools needed to enhance governance, promote transparency, and foster inclusive development.

Our Core Values

  • Excellence: Commitment to delivering high-quality training and consulting services.
  • Integrity: Upholding ethical standards and transparency in all operations.
  • Inclusivity: Promoting gender equality and ensuring accessibility for all participants.
  • Innovation: Continuously seeking new and effective ways to enhance learning and development.
  • Collaboration: Partnering with clients to achieve their goals and improve governance.

Our Services

  • Parliamentary Training Programs: Customized training for parliamentarians on parliamentary procedures, legislative processes, and leadership skills.
  • Governmental Training Programs: Specialized training for government officials on public financial management, ethics, and strategic planning.
  • Workshops and Seminars: Interactive workshops and seminars on contemporary issues in governance, such as gender-sensitive budgeting and sustainable development goals.
  • Consulting Services: Providing expert consulting services to parliaments and government institutions to improve their operational efficiency and effectiveness.

Our Clients

  • Parliamentary Institutions: Training and consulting for national and regional parliaments.
  • Government Agencies: Professional development for various government departments and agencies.
  • International Organizations: Collaborations with international bodies to promote best practices in governance and parliamentary procedures.
