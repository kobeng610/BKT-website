About BKT

BKT Consultants, Inc. brings over 50 years of combined expertise, offering customized training that aligns with each country's distinct governance landscape. With a strong foundation in leadership, communication, legislative processes, and accountability, BKT also provides specialized workshops on gender-sensitive budgeting, public financial management, and ethics.

What sets BKT apart is its unmatched expertise in governance capacity-building. With years of experience training professionals across various sectors, BKT ensures that its programs are tailored to each country’s unique governance environment, delivering practical, impactful solutions. The firm’s proven success is evident in its longstanding partnerships with governance experts, former parliamentarians, government agencies, and prestigious American universities, all contributing to measurable improvements in governance practices.

As a US-based consultancy, BKT upholds the highest standards of integrity, inclusivity, and innovation, ensuring that each training program enhances legislative and policymaking skills, strengthens transparency and ethical practices, and builds public trust and accountability. Through its commitment to excellence, BKT stands ready to support your strategic development goals with customized training solutions that drive meaningful change.
